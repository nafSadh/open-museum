Notes:
- `provenance` is `provenance_url` (schema updated since last audit — single field replaces the earlier `wikipedia_url` + fallback pattern).
- `year_start` coverage is the column to watch for chronological-sort correctness. Where `date` > `year_start`, entries have a date string the parser refused (pure unknowns, or future-year outliers).
- "Dups" counts groups of entries sharing a case-insensitive title. For van-gogh, monet, rembrandt these are almost entirely legitimate (catalogue-raisonné variants, series paintings); the `title_disambig` field handles them at render time.
- Ukiyo-e and several non-Western collections intentionally omit `dimensions` / `current_location` — structurally inapplicable to prints that exist as multiple impressions across museums, or to works whose current holdings are poorly documented.
