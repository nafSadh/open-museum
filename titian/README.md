# Titian Collection

## Scope

164 works by **Tiziano Vecellio / Titian** (c. 1488–1576), the Venetian Renaissance master whose career spanned nearly 70 years. The catalog covers the three canonical eras used by most Titian scholarship: `early` (Venetian apprenticeship through the 1520s), `middle` (portraiture commissions and large mythologies, 1520s–1540s), and `late` (the *poesie* cycle for Philip II and the unfinished meditative works up to 1576).

## Copyright Status

**Public Domain Worldwide.** Titian died in August 1576 — 450 years ago.

## Collection specifics

- **Subject filters**: portrait (largest group), religious, mythological, allegory — useful for navigating a career split roughly evenly between patronage categories.
- **Dimensions + location**: present for 97–99% of entries. Gaps correspond to lost or disputed works.
- **Date notation**: most Titian works carry `"c. YYYY"` in art-historical literature; preserved verbatim in `date` with structured `year_start` / `year_end` / `circa` for sorting.
