# Utagawa Kuniyoshi Collection

## Scope

29 works by **Utagawa Kuniyoshi** (1798–1861), the late-Edo ukiyo-e artist known for dynamic warrior prints, cats, and fantastical scenes of legend and folklore. This is a curated selection from a much larger output (~10,000 prints attributed); covers his *suikoden* warrior series, cat-personification prints, and representative landscapes.

## Copyright Status

**Public Domain Worldwide.** Kuniyoshi died in April 1861 — 164 years ago.

## Collection specifics

- **Technique**: recorded for all 29 (woodblock print / nishiki-e).
- **No `dimensions`, no `current_location`**: ukiyo-e prints exist in multiple impressions; see [AGENTS.md](../AGENTS.md) schema policy.
- **Sparse dates**: Kuniyoshi's prints are often dated by censor seals rather than explicit years. Where the seal fixes a range, `year_start` / `year_end` reflect it; otherwise null.
