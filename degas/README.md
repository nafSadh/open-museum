# Edgar Degas Collection

## Scope

51 works by **Edgar Degas** (1834–1917), the French Impressionist whose reputation rests on dancers, horse-racing scenes, bathers, and psychologically charged portraits. This is a curated selection rather than a complete catalog — Degas was prolific (1,500+ works) and most are held in private collections with limited digital availability.

## Copyright Status

**Public Domain Worldwide.** Degas died in September 1917 — 108 years ago.

## Collection specifics

- **Subject filters**: `dance` (15 works), `nude` (bathers, 8 works), `portrait` (8), `racing` (4) — the four areas Degas is canonically associated with.
- **Era buckets**: `early` (pre-Impressionist history paintings), `impressionist` (the mature 1870s–1890s work), `late` (pastels and wax sculptures).
- **Sparse dimensions**: only 8 of 51 entries carry `dimensions`. Degas worked across oil, pastel, monotype, and sculpture; the source Wikipedia gallery page doesn't uniformly record measurements.
