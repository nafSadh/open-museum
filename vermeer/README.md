# Johannes Vermeer Collection

## Scope

37 works by **Johannes Vermeer** (1632–1675), the Delft master of domestic interiors and light. This is effectively the complete surviving catalog: 34 universally accepted paintings plus a small number of disputed/attributed works.

## Copyright Status

**Public Domain Worldwide.** Vermeer died in December 1675 — 350+ years ago — placing every work far beyond any jurisdiction's copyright term.

## Collection specifics

- **Complete catalog**: Vermeer is one of the few major painters whose entire known output fits in a single README. Completeness is the norm here, not the exception.
- **Rich metadata**: `dimensions`, `current_location`, `technique` are populated for every entry.
- **Date notation**: art-historical uncertainty is preserved in `date` (e.g. `"1654–55 | or c. 1654–56 or c. 1655"`). Use `year_start` / `year_end` / `circa` for any UI sort or filter.
