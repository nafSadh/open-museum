# Rembrandt van Rijn Collection

## Scope

806 works by **Rembrandt van Rijn** (1606–1669) — the Dutch master of light, shadow, and psychological depth. The catalog spans all three media he worked in at scale: 348 paintings, 289 etchings, and 169 drawings. Subjects range from self-portraits (90+ across his career) and religious scenes through group portraits and landscapes.

## Copyright Status

**Public Domain Worldwide.** Rembrandt died in October 1669 — 356+ years ago.

## Collection specifics

- **Three media**: `medium` is populated for all 806 entries (painting / etching / drawing).
- **Technique detail**: 517 entries have a more specific `technique` field (e.g. *Oil on panel*, *Etching with drypoint*).
- **Era buckets**: `early` (Leiden and early Amsterdam), `middle` (the 1630s–1640s prosperity), `late` (post-bankruptcy expressive works).
- **Self-portrait density**: Rembrandt's 90+ self-portraits appear across all eras; use `subject=portrait` + artist-name filter in UI to surface them.
